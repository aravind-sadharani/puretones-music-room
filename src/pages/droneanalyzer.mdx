## Drone Analyzer
Drone Analyzer is a tool that analyzes all the different harmonics and combination tones emitted by a [Drone](/learn/tanpuraworking-1/) and visualizes the overall tuning system induced by the given Drone tuning. Use the tabs below to set the analysis parameters and the tunings of the drone and the scale you wish to analyze.
